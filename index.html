TDEE and Calorie Deficit Calculator

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body burns at rest. TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the number of calories your body burns daily, including activity.

BMR and TDEE Equations

BMR (Male): 66 + (6.23 * weight in lbs) + (12.7 * height in inches) - (6.8 * age)

BMR (Female): 655 + (4.35 * weight in lbs) + (4.7 * height in inches) - (4.7 * age)

TDEE: BMR * Activity Level
