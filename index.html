BMR and TDEE Equations

BMR (Male): 66 + (6.23 * weight in lbs) + (12.7 * height in inches) - (6.8 * age)

BMR (Female): 655 + (4.35 * weight in lbs) + (4.7 * height in inches) - (4.7 * age)

TDEE: BMR * Activity Level
